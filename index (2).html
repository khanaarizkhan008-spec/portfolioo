Student Life

HostelMate

Problem
Students living away from home struggle with homesickness, daily life skills, and building a safe social circle in a new environment.

HostelMate is a comprehensive digital survival kit designed for students adjusting to hostel life. It integrates a native-language AI companion to emotionally support users and reduce homesickness. The platform provides practical video tutorials for everyday tasks such as laundry, cooking basics, and self-care. A hyper-local social navigator helps students safely connect with like-minded peers and explore their surroundings confidently — combining emotional support, life-skill guidance, and social discovery into one seamless experience.

AcademiFlow

Problem
Students often fail to manage their academic time effectively, leading to poor performance despite putting in effort.

AcademiFlow is a premium academic time management platform built to help students take control of their study routines. Designed with an organic brutalist UI, it goes beyond traditional GPA tracking by enabling users to plan, execute, and analyze their study schedules. The platform intelligently correlates planned study hours with actual academic performance, providing insights into productivity patterns and efficiency — transforming time management into a measurable, strategic process.
